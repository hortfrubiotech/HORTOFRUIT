» Genetic modification of avocado (Persea americana) plants

 

The avocado tree orchads in the Mediterraneum coast are specially prone to become infected with the soil fungus Rosellinia necatrix. We have optimized a method to obtain tolerant avocado trees by the in vitro regeneration from somatic embriogenesis that is currenly used to generate plant material expressing proteins that conferer resistence againts the pathogen.
