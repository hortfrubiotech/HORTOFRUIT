» Olive (Olea europaea) regeneration and genetic transformation

 

Olive is a woody species that present serious problems to regenerate and transform. Our research team had set up an in vitro regeneration system by means of somatic embryogenesis, as well as a genetic transformation system starting from an embryogenic callus. Using this system, we're evaluating the role of some olive genes in oil quality. We're also working in the production of plants with enhanced tolerance to fungal pathogens.
