» Paulownia sp micropopagation

 

Plants of genus Paulownia have an important economic potential thus the diversity of uses and applications. They usually are fast growing tress, specially suited for reforestation programs, their wood is considered of high quality, the pulp can be used in paper production and leaves are a good biomass source. In colaboration with "Plantas Continental", a company based in Córdoba, Spain, we have developed protocols for the in vitro micropopagation of several Paulownia clones that allow the production of a high number of plant in a short period of time.
